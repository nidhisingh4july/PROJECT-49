Interesting facts about chameleon

  • Chameleon are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about camel

  • Camel's ears are furry.
  • Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet.
  • When they find water ,they will drink as much as possible.

Interesting facts about cicada

  • Cicadas are best known for their extraordinary, highly synchronized life cycles.
  • Cicada climbs out of its molted skin on a leaf covered by other molted skins.
  • Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica.

Interesting facts about panda

  • They have great camouflage for their environment.
  • Pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo.
  • Pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.

Interesting facts about pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are Spiritual to Some Religions.
  • Pigeons have much better hearing than humans and can hear very low frequencies that we cannot.

Interesting facts about teddy

  • The first toy stuffed teddy bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  • The teddy bear helps to stimulate and develop their imagination.
  • Teddy bears encourage this type of imaginative play by their very nature, in a way a gadget or electronic toy can't.
